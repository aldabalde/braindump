# IoT Definição

"""The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the interconnection of uniquely identifiable embedded computing-like devices within the existing Internet infrastructure. Typically, IoT is expected to offer advanced connectivity of devices, systems, and services that goes beyond machine-to-machine communications (M2M) and covers a variety of protocols, domains, and applications.[1] The interconnection of these embedded devices (including smart objects), is expected to usher in automation in nearly all fields, while also enabling advanced applications like a Smart Grid.[2]"""
<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_of_Things" > IoT Wikipedia</a>

# Características
- Poder Computacional Anywhere
- Um dos vértices da Terceira pLataforma, junto com Cloud e BigData
- Equipamentos "baratos" com inteligência nas pontas
- Voltada para o Hardware, vai além dos smartphones
- Caminho para a integtração maior entre Homem e máquina
